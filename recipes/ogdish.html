RICE AND EGGS

Description

Rice and Eggs will never let you down, unless you have an aversion to rice, or eggs. This isn't your auntie's fried rice either. Rice and eggs holds it down, because it's so simple to make, but you don't need a ton of ingredients to make it!

This recipe is intended for the lazy people who don't know what to make for dinner, and still can't make a decision. Rice and eggs provides natural fats, a quick source of carbohydrates, and a good source of protein. This dish can also be dressed up with various garnishes, however, this recipe is simplified for the basic meal. THIS RECIPE ASSUMES YOU HAVE A RICE COOKER!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • water (The appropriate amount of water for my rice cooker having people)
  • 2 teaspoons of oil
  • 2 eggs

Cooking Directions

  1. Wash your rice with water, and drain the water. Repeat until the water is clear
  2. Proceed to cook your rice after it has been washed.
  3. While the rice is cooking, begin heating a pan on medium heat, and toss in the oil.
  4. Once the oil has heated up, crack the two eggs into the pan, and cook the egg to your desired doneness.
  5. After the egg and rice have been cooked, place the rice into a bowl, and place the egg on top of the rice.
